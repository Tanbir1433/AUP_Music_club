Club Membership Rules & Regulations

To ensure a respectful and productive environment, all members of the Music Club are expected to follow these rules and guidelines.

1. Respect and Conduct

  • Be respectful to all members and club coordinators.
  • No form of discrimination or harassment is tolerated.
  • Use equipment and spaces responsibly.

2. Attendance and Punctuality

  • Members must attend at least 2 club activities per month.
  • Late arrivals may not be allowed to join ongoing sessions.
  • Notify in advance if you cannot attend a scheduled session.

3. Equipment Usage

  • Handle all instruments and devices with care.
  • Return borrowed items on time.
  • Report any damages immediately to the club in-charge.

4. Practice Room Policy

  • Practice sessions must be booked in advance.
  • Keep the room clean and organized after use.
  • Maximum practice time is 2 hours per day per member.

5. Event Participation

  • Participation in at least one major event per semester is mandatory.
  • Respect event schedules and rehearsal timelines.
  • Collaborate with peers during group performances.

6. Communication & Updates

  • Stay updated through official club channels (email/WhatsApp).
  • Respond promptly to club announcements and notices.
  • Use respectful language in all communications.

7. Membership Dues & ID

  • Annual membership fee must be paid before deadline.
  • Carry your club ID during official events and meetings.
  • Lost ID cards must be reported and replaced.

8. Disciplinary Actions

  • Repeated violations may lead to temporary suspension.
  • Serious misconduct can result in termination of membership.
  • Members have the right to appeal disciplinary decisions.

1

Respect and Inclusion

All members must maintain a respectful and inclusive environment. Discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind is strictly prohibited.

2

Active Participation

Members are expected to actively engage in rehearsals, meetings, and events. Minimum participation of two sessions per month is required.

3

Instrument Use

Handle all instruments and studio equipment with care. Always return equipment to its proper place and report any damage immediately.

4

Studio Booking

Members must book studio time in advance via the official schedule. Usage is limited to 2 hours/day to ensure equal access for all.

5

Event Commitment

Participation in club-organized events (e.g., Open Stage, Workshops) is encouraged. Members must attend at least one major event per semester.

6

Communication

Stay updated via our WhatsApp group and email. Members are responsible for checking club communications and responding when needed.
